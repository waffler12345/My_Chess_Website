Chess Instruction and Events in Pittsburgh

Last updated October 17, 2022

My name is Gabriel Petesch and I am a Fide Chess Master (FM) and Professional Chess Instructor from Pittsburgh. I am currently ranked 7th in the state of Pennsylvania.

I have taught private lessons for over 10 years, and have been a full-time Chess Teacher for over 5 years.

Tournaments, Camps, Classes, and Other Events

I am currently running events in a wide variety of locations in the greater-Pittsburgh area, including: Fox Chapel, Mt. Lebanon, McCandless, and Sewickley.

2022 Greater Pittsburgh Scholastic Chess Championship

On May 28, I ran (along with my friend Jules Coulson) a scholastic chess tournament at St. John’s Lutheran Church in McCandless. The tournament was a great success! We had:

  • 45 total players in 3 sections
  • 15 players total competed in their 1st ever USCF rated event
  • 10 of 17 players in the K-4 section competed in their 1st ever USCF rated event!

I plan to continue running USCF tournaments every month or two. My next tournament will be this September 17 at the Good Shephard Lutheran Church in Fox Chapel.
