My Current Chess Events

  • Tournaments.
    On May 28, 2022, I collaborated with Jules Coulson, to run the Greater Pittsburgh Scholastic Chess Championship. Our tournament had a great turnout of 45 competitors, including 15 first-time tournament players!
  • Classes
    I run classes in and around Pittsburgh. Currently I only have one class running in Mt. Lebanon, but more will be coming soon!
  • Simultaneous Exhibitions
    During Simultaneous exhibitions (simuls), I take on many challengers all at once by walking around the room and making a move on one board at a time. Simuls are fun, free, and instructive, and they offer a unique chance to play a Chess Master.
  • After School Clubs
    After school chess classes are a fun way for kids to learn and get more involved in chess. I currently have a few slots open for after-school classes. If you are a parent or school administrator, get in touch to learn more about setting up school classes.
  • Private Lessons
    I have been teaching private students for 10 years. I offer both in-person and skype sessions. Click here to send me a message regarding personal instruction.
